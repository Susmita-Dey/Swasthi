    Third Eye Chakra

    The third eye chakra, also known as Ajna chakra, is located in the center of the forehead and is associated with the pineal gland. This chakra is responsible for intuition, wisdom, and spiritual insight. When this chakra is balanced, one can experience heightened levels of intuition, clarity and perception. An imbalance in this chakra can lead to feelings of confusion, lack of direction, and difficulty in making decisions. The color associated with the third eye chakra is purple, and the element is light. The symbol for this chakra is the lotus with two petals. The third eye chakra is associated with the sense of sight and the ability to see beyond the physical realm. Activating and balancing this chakra can help to improve psychic abilities, enhance intuition and also helps in reducing anxiety and depression. In order to balance this chakra, meditation, yoga, and visualization can be helpful.
