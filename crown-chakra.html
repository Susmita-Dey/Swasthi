    Crown Chakra!

    The crown chakra, also known as Sahasrara, is located at the top of the head. It is associated with spiritual enlightenment and connection to the divine. The color associated with the crown chakra is violet or white. Imbalances in the crown chakra can lead to feelings of disconnection and a lack of purpose. When balanced, the crown chakra allows for a sense of inner peace and understanding of one's place in the world. It is associated with the pituitary gland and the central nervous system. The crown chakra is the seventh and highest chakra in the body. Activating this chakra can bring a sense of oneness with the universe. It is associated with the pineal gland and the nervous system.
